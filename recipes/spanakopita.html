Spanakopita

Description

Spanakopita is a famous traditional Greek recipe which consists of a phyllo dough based pie stuffed with spinach and herbs

Ingredients

  • 18 phyllo sheets (kroustas)
  • 2 lb fresh spinach (or frozen spinach), chopped and blanched
  • 6 scallions , finely diced
  • 3 eggs , beaten
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 8 oz. feta cheese
  • 3 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F / 180 C.
  2. Drain the spinach to remove the excess liquid.
  3. Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a pan and saut√© the onions and spinach over high heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Set aside until cool.
  4. Put spinach and onions in a large bowl, add dill and parsley and mix well by mashing with the hands or with a potato masher. The volume is reduced by half.
  5. Add remaining ingredients, except olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, and mix well. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Add one filo sheet. Brush with oil and place a second sheet and so on until the ninth.
  7. Spoon the spinach mixture over the entire surface.
  8. Place a sheet of phyllo dough, oiled lightly and proceed in the same way for all the remaining sheets.
  9. Fold the edges and oil the surface of the last sheet. Press on the pie. Draw portions with a knife at the surface.
  10. Bake for about thirty minutes, until golden brown.
  11. To get a crispy dough that may have softened the next day, bake the leftover pie for a few minutes.
